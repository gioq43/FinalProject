Boeing or Airbus


The World's skies are dominated by thousands of airplanes at any given time. All differnet Types, Makes and Models. There are two Main Aircraft Manufacturers that dominate those skies, Boeing and Airbus. Boeing has had a rich history in the Commercial Passenger Jet Business witht the release of the 707 in 1957. Since then it has come out with several different types of aircraft in the 700 Series that have helped advance the Aviation Industry.





On The other We have Airbus. Airbus is Boeing's main competitor and has developed its own fleet of aircraft since it was first founded in 1970 in France. Since then Airbus has manufactured Industry Changing Airplanes like the Double Decker A380 which was in a class of it own which in a full economoy configuration can fill up to over 800 people. It has also recently developed the brand New A350 which has the ability to fly NonStop for over 20 Hours.
